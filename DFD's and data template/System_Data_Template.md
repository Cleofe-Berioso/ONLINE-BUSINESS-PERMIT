# Chapter III

## Permit and Regulatory Fees

### Article A: Mayor’s Permit Fee on Business

#### Section 1. Imposition
An annual fee shall be collected for the issuance of a Mayor’s Permit to operate a business, pursue an occupation or calling, or conduct any activity within the municipality.

The permit fee applies to every distinct or separate business or place where trade is conducted. However, a line of business that is exempt from tax shall also be exempt from the permit fee, provided the corresponding fee has been properly secured.

### Basis of Classification
For the purpose of determining the Mayor’s Permit Fee, businesses are classified according to:

#### 1. Asset Size
- Below ₱100,000
- ₱100,000 – ₱250,000
- ₱250,000 – ₱500,000
- ₱500,000 – ₱2,000,000
- ₱2,000,000 – ₱5,000,000
- ₱5,000,000 – ₱20,000,000
- Over ₱20,000,000

#### 2. Number of Workers
- None
- 1–5
- 6–10
- 11–50
- 51–99
- 100–150
- 200 or more

**Note:** The permit fee shall be based on either asset size or number of workers—whichever results in a higher fee.
